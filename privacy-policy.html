Privacy Policy

Pokémon Quiz Game

👋 Overview

Welcome to the Pokémon Quiz Game. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. The short version: we collect very little, and we never sell your data.

This game is a fan-made project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Nintendo, Game Freak, or The Pokémon Company.

📦 Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

The following data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted:

  • Game preferences and settings you choose

When you use voice input, the app also collects and transmits the following data to a private Google Sheet for the purpose of improving speech recognition accuracy:

  • The text transcript of what the speech recognizer heard
  • Confidence scores and related metadata returned by the speech engine

Audio is never recorded or stored. Only the text output of the speech recognition engine is logged. This data is not linked to any personal identifier.

🚫 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or other personal identifiers
  • Raw audio recordings
  • Location data
  • Contacts or other device data
  • Payment information

🌐 Third-Party Services

Apple Speech Recognition: Voice input is processed by iOS's built-in speech recognition engine, which may transmit audio to Apple's servers for processing. This is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Google Sheets: Speech recognition transcripts and confidence scores are logged to a private Google Sheet accessible only to the developer. This data is used solely to improve voice recognition accuracy in future updates and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

PokéAPI: The app fetches Pokémon data from PokéAPI, a free public API. PokéAPI does not collect any personally identifiable information.

If you download the app through the Apple App Store, Apple may collect device and usage information under their own privacy policies.

👶 Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your region). The voice recognition logs we collect contain no personal identifiers. We believe the app is fully compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

🔒 Data Security

Game preferences are stored locally on your device and protected by its built-in security. Voice recognition logs sent to Google Sheets are stored in a private spreadsheet accessible only to the developer and are not shared with any third parties.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
