Privacy Policy

Pokémon Quiz Game

👋 Overview

Welcome to the Pokémon Quiz Game. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. The short version: we collect very little, and we never sell your data.

This game is a fan-made project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Nintendo, Game Freak, or The Pokémon Company.

📦 Information We Collect Minimal

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

The app may store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your high scores and quiz progress
  • Game preferences and settings you choose

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

🚫 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or other personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Contacts or other device data
  • Payment information
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent off-device

🌐 Third-Party Services

The app fetches Pokémon data from PokéAPI, a free and open Pokémon data API. PokéAPI does not collect any personally identifiable information from users of apps that access it. You can review their terms at pokeapi.co.

If you download the app through the Apple App Store or Google Play, Apple and Google may collect device and usage information under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies separately.

👶 Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your region). Because we collect no personal data at all, we believe the app is fully compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

🔒 Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security. We do not transmit your data to external servers, so there is no server-side risk.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
